How To Use Pop It?

A Pop-It is a fidget toy consisting of a usually-brightly colored silicone tray with pokable bubbles,
similar to bubble wrap, that can be flipped and re-used. They come in a variety of colors, shapes, and sizes,
and are marketed as a stress-reliever
